Formulas for geometry:

Perimeter of a Square = 4 × Side.

Perimeter of a Rectangle = 2 × (Length + Breadth)

Area of a Square = Side²

Area of a Rectangle = Length × Breadth.

Area of a Triangle = ½ × Base × Height.

Area of a Circle = A = π × r²
